TransformED Fellowship

TransformED is a two-year professional programme that is designed to develop the youth of Tamil Nadu into Change Facilitators who will enable schools to function better as an eco-system. As a full-time resource at the school, the fellow focuses on whole school transformation, leadership development of school leaders and professional development of teachers.

The fellowship will start accepting applications from late 2019.

The Model

The fellowship aims to build an eco-system within the school to enable school leaders (HMs), teachers and school processes to be centeredaround holistic development of the students.

While working on their personal transformation, the fellow will also be on a journey to develop a deep understanding of the system and internal and external influences. Other areas of development include problem identification, system thinking and change management, influencing without authority, and resource optimisation.

The fellowship assists the fellows to become well-groomed, responsible social entrepreneurs. This is an opportunity for those who are seeking to transform the school education system while transforming themselves. During this period, fellows will understand how schools are run and how the quality of education provided for the child can be improved. After the two-year fellowship, every fellow is given an opportunity to lead 5-8 schools as a cluster lead or replicate the model (in his/her hometown) – triggering a snowball effect of transformational education.

Selection Process

Three stages to help us understand each other better

Apply

Complete the application form to give us a sense of why you want to be a part of the fellowship

Telephonic Interview

Connect over a call to understand your leadership skills, ability and acumen

Personal Interview

Interact with other applicants through a group discussion and participate in the final interview

Aspiring Applicants

We are looking for vibrant young graduates who can read, write and speak Tamil fluently and are willing to commit to a 24-month full-time fellowship in Chennai.

The fellowship will start accepting applications from late 2019.

About Us

The fellowship is jointly run by Bhumi and Vidhya Vidhai, two organisations striving to improve the quality of education in the country.

Vision: To build an ecosystem that is centred around the holistic development of children

Mission: To transform schools as fearless, nurturing, creative, and safe learning spaces by enabling youth as agents of change

Core Values: Collaboration | Humility | Ownership | Integrity | Sustainability | Empathy
